Code-Coach

Code-Coach is a coaching program for adults dreaming of a career switch to programming.

Coachees with difficulties in a subject and who want to make progress can ask for explanations and advice to coaches by requesting a session.

With Code-Coach everything is quick and easy: just register as a Coach or request a coaching session on this website, and it will organize a private coaching session.
